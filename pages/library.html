Rules

1. Each student must carry his/ her Reference card or Lending card to the library. Without these cards student will not be allowed to avail the various services of Central Library. 2. Students can use reading room. Reading rrom is open form 10 am to 2 pm and 3 pm to 5pm. 3. Student can borrow book only for 2 weeks. After 2 weeks, Rs 1 per day should be charge as fine. 4. Book issue time is at 9 am to 11am and student can collect their book from 12 pm to 5 pm. 5. Only present students, teachers and staff of the college can avail the library services. Please inform the college if you lost the card and make a FIR nearest Police Station. 8. Students must return all books before form fill up for the University examination and get the no due certificate from the Central Library. 9. If books are lost or damaged, borrower must replace a copy of the book or the latest edition available in the market. 10. Damaging library property is a serious offence.
